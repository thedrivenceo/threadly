## Overview

The PublishMuse Chrome Extension lets you save content to your Inspiration board without leaving the page you're browsing. It works in two ways:

- **On any website**: Click the extension icon to save the current page URL with an optional pillar and content type
- **On threads.net**: A bookmark icon appears on each post for instant one-click saving

## Installation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the extension" icon="download" title-type="p">
    Download the extension folder from your PublishMuse account or the Chrome Web Store.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Load in Chrome" icon="chrome" title-type="p">
    Open `chrome://extensions` in Chrome, enable **Developer mode** (top right toggle), and click **Load unpacked**. Select the extension folder.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect your account" icon="link" title-type="p">
    Click the PublishMuse icon in your Chrome toolbar. You need two pieces of information from your Settings page:

    1. **Webhook URL**: Copy from Settings > Webhook
    2. **Webhook Secret**: Copy from Settings > Webhook (click the eye icon to reveal it)

    Paste both into the extension and click **Connect**. The extension will test the connection and confirm it worked.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">
  The extension validates your credentials before saving them. If you see an error, double check that you copied the full webhook URL and secret without any extra spaces.
</Callout>

## Saving from any page

1. Navigate to any page you want to save as inspiration
2. Click the PublishMuse icon in your toolbar
3. The current page URL is auto-filled
4. Optionally select a **Pillar** and **Content Type** from the dropdowns
5. Click **Save to Inspiration**

Your categories (pillars and content types) are fetched fresh from your account every time you open the popup, so any changes you make in the app are immediately reflected.

## Saving from Threads

When you browse `threads.net`, the extension automatically adds a bookmark icon to each post in your feed.

- Click the bookmark icon to instantly save the post URL to your Inspiration board
- The icon fills in to confirm the save was successful
- The post text is captured alongside the URL

<Callout kind="tip">
  This is the fastest way to build your inspiration library. Scroll through Threads and bookmark anything that catches your eye, then repurpose it later using the AI tools in PublishMuse.
</Callout>

## Changing your connection

Click **Settings** in the bottom right of the extension popup to update your webhook URL or secret. This is useful if you regenerate your webhook secret or switch accounts.

## How it works

The extension communicates with PublishMuse through the `save-inspiration` edge function. Every request is authenticated using your unique webhook secret, so only you can save to your Inspiration board.

The flow:
1. Extension sends the URL, pillar, and content type to the edge function
2. Edge function validates your webhook secret
3. Content is saved to the `inspirations` table in your account
4. The entry appears on your Inspiration page in PublishMuse

## Troubleshooting

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="Extension shows 'Invalid webhook secret'" default-open="false">
    - Go to Settings in PublishMuse and reveal your webhook secret
    - Click the copy button to copy it exactly
    - In the extension, click Settings, clear the secret field, and paste fresh
    - Make sure there are no extra spaces before or after the secret
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Extension shows 'Could not reach the server'" default-open="false">
    - Check your internet connection
    - Verify the Webhook URL is correct (copy it fresh from Settings)
    - The URL should look like `https://xxxxx.supabase.co/functions/v1/webhook-create-post`
    - The extension automatically extracts the base URL, so pasting the full webhook URL is fine
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Bookmark button doesn't appear on Threads" default-open="false">
    - Make sure you're on `threads.net` (not a different domain)
    - Try refreshing the page
    - Check that the extension is enabled in `chrome://extensions`
    - The button appears on posts as they load, including during infinite scroll
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="Categories not showing in dropdowns" default-open="false">
    - Categories are fetched from your account when the popup opens
    - Make sure you have pillars and content types set up on your Categories page
    - Try clicking Settings and reconnecting
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>
